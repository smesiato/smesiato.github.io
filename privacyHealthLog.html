Privacy Policy for HealthLog

Information Collected

HealthLog collects step counts, fitness activities, hear rate information, and sleep data from your fitness tracker. HealthLog also collects information about your caloric intake, weight metrics, and body composition metrics.

Information Usuage

the information collected by HealthLog is used to anaylze your daily fitness activity, your daily caloric intake, and provide an anlaysis report only shared with the user directly. This information is not shared with any other persons, or third parties and is striclty accessible by the user directly.

Information Storage

the information collected by HealthLog is stored in a secure database only accessible via API calls which are authorized by an authenticated user. The authenticated user will only have access to there data and will not be able to access other users data.
